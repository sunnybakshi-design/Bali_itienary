Bali Instagram Itinerary

October 2 – October 13, 2025

Day 1 (Oct 2): Arrival & Southern Kickoff

Morning (08:00–11:00): Land at Ngurah Rai International Airport. Transfer to Seminyak (approx. 12–15 km, 30–45 minutes). Check-in at W Bali – Seminyak or The Legian Bali.

Afternoon (12:00–17:00): Lunch at Bambu Restaurant, stroll along Seminyak Beach, and enjoy boutique shops and street art. Coffee break at Revolver Espresso in Canggu.

Evening (18:00–21:00): Drive to Tanah Lot (approx. 20 km, 45 minutes) for sunset photos. Dinner at La Lucciola in Petitenget. Return to Seminyak.

Day 2 (Oct 3): South Bali – Cliffs, Culture & Cuisine

Morning (07:00–10:00): Breakfast at your hotel, drive to Uluwatu (25 km, 45 minutes), and visit Uluwatu Temple.

Afternoon (10:30–15:00): Lunch at Single Fin Café, then relax at Padang Padang or Dreamland Beach.

Evening (15:30–21:00): Enjoy the Kecak Fire Dance at Uluwatu during sunset. Dinner at Menega Café in Jimbaran (approx. 20 km, 45 minutes drive). Overnight in South Bali.

Day 3 (Oct 4): Transition to Central Bali – Ubud

Morning (07:00–10:00): Breakfast at your South Bali hotel and drive to Ubud (35 km, approx. 1 hour). Check in at Maya Ubud Resort & Spa or Alila Ubud.

Late Morning & Afternoon (10:00–16:00): Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest, have lunch at Clear Café Ubud, explore the Ubud Art Market and Palace, and capture the Tegalalang Rice Terraces.

Evening (16:30–21:00): Dinner at Locavore or a local warung. Overnight in Ubud.

Day 4 (Oct 5): Ubud’s Culture & Adventure

Early Morning (04:30–09:00): Mount Batur Sunrise Trek (pickup at 03:30–04:00; drive 1.5–2 hours to base; 2-hour trek). Breakfast atop Mount Batur.

Late Morning (10:30–12:00): Relax at Toya Devasya Hot Springs.

Afternoon (12:30–16:00): Visit Tirta Empul Temple (approx. 20 km, 45 minutes drive) and have lunch at Ibu Oka in central Ubud.

Evening (16:30–21:00): Stroll along the Campuhan Ridge Walk, dinner at Swept Away at The Samaya Ubud. Overnight in Ubud.

Day 5 (Oct 6): Northern Bali – Temples & Waterfalls

Morning (07:00–10:00): Breakfast in Ubud, drive to Bedugul (approx. 70 km, 1.5–2 hours). Visit Ulun Danu Beratan Temple (10:00–11:30).

Late Morning & Afternoon (11:30–16:00): Lunch at Pondok Indah Restaurant in Bedugul. Visit Gitgit Waterfall (approx. 30 km, 45 minutes drive; visit 16:00–17:00).

Evening (17:30–21:00): Dinner at a mountain-view warung (e.g., Warung Dewa Ruci). Overnight in Bedugul (e.g., Handara Golf & Resort Bali).

Day 6 (Oct 7): Northern Bali – Munduk & Lovina

Morning (07:00–09:00): Breakfast in Bedugul and drive to Munduk (approx. 20 km, 40 minutes). Visit Munduk Waterfall (09:00–10:30) and nearby coffee plantations.

Late Morning & Early Afternoon (10:30–13:00): Drive to Lovina (approx. 30 km, 1 hour). Lunch at Spice Beach Club in Lovina.

Afternoon (13:00–16:00): Explore Lovina Beach; consider planning a dolphin tour for the next morning.

Evening (16:30–20:30): Dinner at The Lovina Bali Resort Restaurant or a beachfront warung. Overnight in Lovina or nearby (e.g., Puri Lumbung Cottages).

Day 7 (Oct 8): East Bali – Lempuyang & Tirta Gangga

Morning (06:30–10:00): Early drive from Lovina to East Bali (approx. 80 km, 2.5 hours). Check in at Candi Beach Resort & Spa in Karangasem/Candidasa.

Late Morning (10:30–12:00): Visit Lempuyang Temple (Gates of Heaven) for iconic photo opportunities.

Afternoon (12:00–15:00): Lunch at Warung Dewa Malen near Candidasa; visit Tirta Gangga Water Palace (approx. 25 km, 40 minutes drive).

Evening (15:30–21:00): Explore coastal villages; dinner at Kai Kitchen & Bar in Candidasa. Overnight in East Bali.

Day 8 (Oct 9): Nusa Penida – Island Adventure

Morning (06:30–07:00): Depart from East Bali (or a South Bali hotel) to Sanur (approx. 100 km, 2–2.5 hours drive). Take a speedboat from Sanur to Nusa Penida (30–45 minute ride).

Full Day (08:30–17:00): Explore Nusa Penida highlights: Kelingking Beach, Angel’s Billabong, Broken Beach, and Crystal Bay. Lunch at a beachfront warung on the island.

Evening (17:30–21:00): Return by speedboat to Sanur and drive to South Bali (approx. 30 km, 45 minutes). Overnight in South Bali.

Day 9 (Oct 10): Extra South Bali – Day 1

Morning (07:00–09:00): Breakfast at your South Bali hotel (The Seminyak Beach Resort & Spa or Ayana Resort and Spa, Jimbaran). Enjoy some pool or beach time.

Afternoon (09:30–16:00): Lunch at Biku in Seminyak. Explore Seminyak/Canggu streets, cafés, and local markets. Capture murals and boutique scenes.

Evening (16:30–21:00): Sunset at Jimbaran Beach; dinner at Menega Café. Overnight in South Bali.

Day 10 (Oct 11): South Bali – Uluwatu & Beach Club Leisure

Morning (07:00–10:00): Breakfast at your resort and drive to Uluwatu (approx. 25 km, 45 minutes). Visit Uluwatu Temple for early morning photos.

Afternoon (10:30–16:00): Lunch at Single Fin in Uluwatu; relax at Padang Padang or Dreamland Beach. Optionally enjoy a spa session.

Evening (16:30–21:00): Return to Uluwatu for the Kecak Fire Dance at sunset; dinner at Sundays Beach Club or a clifftop restaurant. Overnight in Uluwatu/Jimbaran.

Day 11 (Oct 12): Extra South Bali – Day 2

Morning (08:00–11:00): Brunch at Crate Café in Canggu; take a photo walk exploring street art, boutiques, and surf spots (e.g., Echo Beach).

Afternoon (11:30–16:00): Lunch at Betelnut Café in Canggu; relax at an Instagram-famous beach club like La Laguna or The Lawn.

Evening (16:30–21:00): Enjoy a farewell sunset at Jimbaran or Uluwatu; dinner at Cuca Restaurant in Jimbaran. Overnight in South Bali.

Day 12 (Oct 13): Departure

Morning (08:00–11:00): Breakfast at your resort or local café; enjoy a final beach stroll or souvenir shopping.

Afternoon (11:00–13:00): Check-out and transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport (approx. 30–45 minutes) for your departure flight.
